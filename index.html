Privacy Policy — Phone Anti-Theft Alarm

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Overview

Phone Anti-Theft Alarm is a utility app that protects your phone from theft by triggering alarms when your charger is unplugged, your phone is moved, or your battery reaches a target level.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No browsing history
  • No contacts or messages
  • No photos or videos (camera is used ONLY for flashlight strobe)
  • No audio recording without your explicit action (Pro custom sounds are stored locally on your device)
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising SDKs that track you
  • No cloud storage or backend servers

Permissions Explained

PermissionWhy We Need It
Foreground ServiceKeeps alarm monitoring alive when your phone is locked or the app is in the background
CameraFlashes the camera light as a visual deterrent (strobe). We do NOT take photos or videos.
VibrateAdds physical vibration to the alarm
NotificationsShows you whether the alarm is armed or disarmed
Do Not Disturb AccessEnsures the siren plays at full volume even when your phone is on silent
Record AudioOnly used when YOU choose to record a custom alarm sound (Pro feature). Audio is stored locally on your device. Never recorded without your action.
Receive Boot CompletedAuto re-arms the alarm after your phone restarts
Device Admin (optional)Prevents the app from being uninstalled while the alarm is armed (your choice)

Data Stored Locally

All settings are stored on your device using Android's local storage (SharedPreferences):

  • Selected alarm mode
  • Volume, vibration, and strobe preferences
  • PIN (stored as a cryptographic hash — we cannot see your actual PIN)
  • Custom sound files (stored in your device's app data directory)
  • Pro unlock status (verified by Google Play, stored locally)

None of this data leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

The Pro unlock ($1.99 one-time) is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a confirmation of purchase from Google — we do not receive or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page. Since we don't collect data, there are no data-handling changes to notify you about.
