Privacy Policy — Flash Alert

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Flash Alert flashes your phone's camera LED when you receive notifications (calls, messages, app alerts) so you notice them in noisy or silent environments.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information, location, browsing history, contacts, or messages
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • No cloud storage or backend servers
  • Notification content is read locally only to determine if flash should trigger — content is never stored, transmitted, or logged
