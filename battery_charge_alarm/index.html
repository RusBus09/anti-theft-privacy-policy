Privacy Policy — Battery Charge Alarm

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Battery Charge Alarm monitors your battery level and alerts you when your phone reaches a target charge percentage or when the charger is unplugged.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information, location, browsing history, contacts, or messages
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • No cloud storage or backend servers
