Privacy Policy — Keep Screen Awake

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Keep Screen Awake keeps your phone screen on for a selected duration so it doesn't sleep while you're reading, cooking, or following instructions.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information, location, browsing history, contacts, or messages
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • No cloud storage or backend servers

Permissions Explained

PermissionWhy
Foreground ServiceKeeps wake lock active when app is backgrounded
Wake LockPrevents screen from sleeping
NotificationsShows active timer with remaining time
Usage Access (Pro)Per-app rules — only keeps screen on when specific apps are open. Read locally only.

Data Stored Locally

Settings stored on device: default duration, Pro status, per-app rules. None leaves your device.
