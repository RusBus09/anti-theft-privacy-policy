Keep Screen Awake
Privacy Policy

Keep Screen Awake keeps your screen on for timed sessions you control.

Package: com.rusbus09.keep_screen_awake Effective date: June 30, 2026

Summary

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Keep Screen Awake has no account system, no developer-operated backend, no ads, and no analytics. The app is designed to run locally on your device.

Local data and settings

Settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only, using Android SharedPreferences. Examples include enabled options, thresholds, timers, alert preferences, or other app settings you choose.

These local settings are not sent to Micro-App Factory or any developer-controlled server.

Permissions and why they are needed

The app requests only the permissions needed to provide its core features:

  • WAKE_LOCK
    Keeps the screen awake only during sessions or rules you start or configure.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS
    Shows active-session status and optional session completion notifications.

In-App Purchases

$0.99 one-time Pro upgrade unlocks per-app rules and scheduled keep-awake sessions.

Purchases are processed by Google Play. Micro-App Factory does not see, collect, or store your payment card number, bank details, or other payment credentials.

Deleting your data

You can delete local app data at any time by clearing the app's data in Android settings or by uninstalling the app.
